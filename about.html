About Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa

Nestled in the heart of Warwickshire, Walton Hall Hotel & Spa blends timeless heritage with contemporary luxury to craft an unforgettable escape of elegance and serenity.

Dating back to the 17th century, Walton Hall has been meticulously restored, preserving its aristocratic charm while infusing modern sophistication for a truly opulent experience.
