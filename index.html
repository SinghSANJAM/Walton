Spa & Leisure

In the tranquil Warwickshire countryside, the Spa sits on the Walton Hall estate, while the health and leisure club is part of the more contemporary part of the property. Offering everything a modern traveller could ask for to work out and wind down, there's a fitness studio, an indoor pool, sauna and steam room, as well as views onto the pretty gardens and grounds outside. In addition to the facilities, the spa offers a range of face and body treatments, manicures and pedicures. There are seven treatment rooms, and therapists perfect for pampering and feeling your best.

✨ Pro Tip: Try our signature hot stone massage for ultimate relaxation!

Getting Here

From the M40 (North), exit at junction 13. Turn left and then take the first right. Continue through Newbold Pacey, then turn left at the T-junction in Wellesbourne.

From the M40 (South), exit at junction 15. Take the fourth exit onto the A429 to Cirencester. Continue through Barford and turn left at the roundabout for Wellesbourne.
